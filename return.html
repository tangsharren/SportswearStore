Return & Refund

We pride ourselves of our products and dedication to customer satisfaction!

To guarantee this, we allow a period of 60 days to return your purchase.

1. Products purchased on the Website may be returned to Deportes for a refund subject to the following:

a) Products must be returned within sixty (60) days from the date of purchase;

b) Products that are deemed to be intentionally damaged or not properly maintained, used outside of their intended means and Products such as foods and drinks, cosmetics, pharmaceutical products, gas canisters, personalised items and personal safety equipments are exempted;


Deportes reserves the right to accept or refuse the return based on our assessment, taking into consideration factors such as the condition of the products and length/frequency of use.

2. The Customer can initiate a request to return the Products by:

Reaching out to the Deportes Customer Service team (contact details can be obtained via the "Contact Us" section on the website)

3. The Customer may return Products purchased online for a refund:

a) at any Deportes Malaysia's physical stores in Malaysia; or

b) by courier.


The Customer will be responsible for the shipping costs of Products returned by courier. The transfer of title and risk of the Products will remain with the Customer until the Products have reached Deportes’s warehouse/store. The Customer must package the item carefully and include the return form leaflet completed with all necessary details. After shipping the Products, the Customer must provide the Deportes Customer Service team with the airway bill or tracking number for traceability purposes.

4. The Products will be inspected once received and in the event the Products are not eligible for a refund pursuant, Deportes may send the Products back to the Customer at the Customer’s cost and/or refuse to provide a refund.

5. Products which are eligible for a return will be processed within fourteen (14) to twenty (20) days after Deportes receives it.

Refunds will be based on the original mode of payment for the Order of the Products.

6. If Deportes has sent you an incorrect or defective item or a Product has been mis-described on the Website, and you would like to return it, either a refund or an exchange will be offered. In this event, Deportes shall bear the cost of return shipping. Please contact the Deportes Customer Service team (contact details can be obtained via the "Contact Us" section on the Website) for further instructions on how to proceed. Provided that the Product is returned with reasonable care having been taken, the refund to be provided by Deportes shall be based on your original mode of payment for the Order of the Product.
