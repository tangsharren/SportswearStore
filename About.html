About Us

DEPORTES is a malaysia sports retail company based in kuala lumpur founded in 2021 by a group of people who are passionate about sports. our goal is to inspire and empower people around the world, by fueling them with a shared passion for sports. we not only strive to unite people in sports but also provide quality goods that are both durable and sustainable. we are passionate about protecting the future of sports. we want to help create a healthy planet where everyone is able to thrive. we sell general sports items such as shoes, clothes pants and accessories that cater to all ages and genders. the products that we chose to put on our shelf are from brands that share the same ideology as us when it comes to mother nature. here we only sell products that use sustainable materials. to name a few sustainable materials in the products we sell: organic cotton, recycled cotton, organic lenin, recycled polyester and so on and so forth.

Vision and Mission

  • Make sports available for all for a healthier and more energized community
  • Always provide the best service to our customer
  • Maintain and always improve our product's quality
